Information We Collect

BioBoost AI is designed with privacy in mind. Here's exactly what we collect:

Information You Provide

When you use our Brand Kit Generator, you enter personal and professional details such as your name, job title, skills, experience level, industry, and goals. This information is used exclusively to generate your branding kit content.

Contact Form Data

If you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content to respond to your inquiry.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect standard web analytics data including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level only)
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To generate your personalized branding kit content
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our service
  • Security: To protect against misuse and ensure platform integrity
Important: We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is yours.
03

Data Storage & Security

Your branding kit is generated entirely in your browser. The professional details you enter into the generator form are not transmitted to our servers and are not stored in any database. All processing happens client-side.

For contact form submissions, we store your message securely and retain it only as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry, after which it is permanently deleted.

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication for internal systems
  • Data minimization — we only collect what we need
04

Cookies & Tracking

BioBoost AI uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., session management)
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage data to help us understand how the site is used (can be opted out)

We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or user profiling. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their terms.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@bioboost-ai.com.
